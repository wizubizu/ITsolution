BRIGHT TOWER NIGERIA LTD

Who We Are


Bright Tower Nigeria ltd, with corporate headquarters located in Lagos was Incorporated in 2011 as a Total ICT Solutions company.

Our solutions and core competencies include Network Infrastructure Solutions: Data and Voice structured cabling, VSAT and Radio solutions, Power Solutions, Surveillance systems, Call Centre Solution, Software Testing and Procurement leveraging on our revered partnerships with our partners and OEMs.

Our aim is to become a major player and an icon in the IT and Telecommunications industry, in the provisioning and building of reliable, innovative, responsive and dependable IT and Telecommunications infrastructures in Nigeria.
